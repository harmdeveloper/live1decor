About us

✅︎DecorVilla is a multi-award winning high-end interior design practice specialising in providing our clients with outstanding luxury interiors across the UK and worldwide.

More than just an Interior Design Studio

At ✅︎DecorVilla we take a holistic approach to look after every aspect of your project, from a complete home redesign, working with council approvals, calling upon an incredible team of builders and tradespeople and furnishing your space.


We understand the technical aspects of building but are also creative with colour, materials and styling. We take the time to get to know you to create a home that tells your story.
