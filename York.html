York River

THE PROJECT

The program for this full remodel incorporated walls of east-facing windows, allowing for beautiful morning light and an evening dance of shadows from the large oaks surrounding the property. At just 1700 square feet, we provided utility in every square inch. The dominant interior tones are blues and greens to connect the eye to the York River and the surrounding mature trees.
