Kittery Point

THE PROJECT

Architectural pieces of the original 1700's waterfront home were salvaged and reconditioned to create a new structure that relates to the site and extensive history of the property. One of the first homes in the town of Kittery, we planned the furnishings and fixtures to incorporate historically significant relics of the property and sourced pieces that complemented the tension in the architecture. Decoration for decoration's sake was eschewed for clean-lined elements and custom furnishings designed to suit the architectural program.
