Binnacle Hill

THE PROJECT

A new construction home in Goose Rocks Beach, ME., our team was tasked with providing an interior program that reflected the traditional elements of the architecture, the beachside location, and the casual lifestyle of the young family inhabiting the home. We incorporated curves (seen in the custom cabinetry, the transitional arches and the lines of the furnishings) as a recurrent design element and incorporated texture through wall coverings, textiles and architectural millwork. This project was a collaboration with McKay Architects and R. Moody and Sons.
