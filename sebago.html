Sebago

THE PROJECT

The marriage of the home's original Mid Century Modern architecture and the nostalgia and natural environment of the Sebago lake location inspired Tyler Karu Design and Interiors to develop a comfortable, livable design plan for a family excited to put down roots in Maine. The scope of work included a to-the-studs remodel and complete redesign of this 1969 lakefront home. Sited on a ridge, the wall-to-wall views of Sebago Lake, the horizon line and treetops allow for an indoor/outdoor connection that inspired the materials palette.
