Ox point

THE PROJECT

A waterfront Mid Century Modern ranch is a rare find in Maine. The TKDI team took Ox Point down to the studs and developed a floor plan to maximize the spacious footprint. The design plan recalls original Mid Century details, including terrazzo floors, walnut casework, and a living room wet bar. Spruce Creek sunrises inspired the outdoor living program, while the jewel tones of the creek were articulated indoors through textiles, tile and rugs.
